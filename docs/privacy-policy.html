Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

Summary: Raw Dawg does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Information We Collect

We collect nothing. Raw Dawg is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • No account required - You don't need to sign up or log in
  • No personal information - We don't ask for your name, email, or any identifiers
  • No analytics - We don't track how you use the app
  • No advertising - We don't show ads or share data with advertisers
  • No third-party tracking - We don't use any SDKs that collect data

Device Permissions

Raw Dawg requests the following permissions, used only for core functionality:

  • Camera: To record your session videos and capture selfie share cards. Videos are processed entirely on your device.
  • Photo Library: To save your session videos and share cards to your camera roll. We only write to your library; we don't read or access other photos.
  • Microphone: Accessed as part of video recording. Audio is included in your saved videos only.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device:

  • Session statistics (total sessions, streaks, time) - Stored in local app storage
  • Videos and photos - Saved to your device's camera roll
  • Preferences - Stored locally on your device

This data never leaves your device. We have no servers, no cloud storage, and no way to access your information.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we don't have access to any data. When you share content to social media, you're using your device's native sharing functionality directly with those platforms.

Children's Privacy

Raw Dawg does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function. If you are a parent and believe your child has somehow provided personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: Your data is in the app and your camera roll
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to remove all app data, or delete videos from your camera roll
  • Portability: Your videos are standard files you can move anywhere

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Effective Date" above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
